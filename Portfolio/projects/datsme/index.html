Uniqueness

Datsme is unique in its feature of Psychological basis and Advanced filtering.

Compatibility at your doorstep.

Datsme is a free mobile app that finds the most compatible people near you and displays them in clusters on a map based UI.

Psychological Basis.

Datsme app is created with the help of psychologists and takes into account the many factors that are responsible in calculating compatibility between two individuals.

Your security is our foremost concern.

Our algorithm tries to group users into clusters so that the exact location of a particular user is not exposed. In addition, the zoom levels of the map are set in such a way that the exact location of the user cannot be deciphered.
